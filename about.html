Company History

East Africa Born Wood Company Ltd

Headquartered in Mafinga, Tanzania (P.O. Box 575), East Africa Born Wood Company Ltd has established itself as a leading manufacturer and supplier of plywood products across the East African region and beyond.

We combine local manufacturing expertise with international trade experience, producing a comprehensive range of plywood from 3mm to 18mm for construction, furniture, and packaging applications. Our strategic position in Tanzania enables us to serve both regional African markets and international buyers with competitive pricing and reliable supply.

  • Manufacturing & trading hub in Mafinga, Tanzania
  • Full product range: 3mm / 6mm / 9mm / 12mm / 15mm / 18mm
  • Construction, furniture, and packaging plywood specialists
  • Export-focused B2B operations
  • Strong supply chain partnerships in China
