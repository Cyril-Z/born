6-Stage Quality Control

From Raw Material to Finished Product

Veneer Inspection

Incoming veneer is inspected for thickness consistency, moisture content, and defect rates. Substandard material is rejected at the gate.

Glue Mixing & Testing

Adhesive formulations are batch-tested for viscosity, solid content, and bonding strength before entering the production line.

Core Assembly

Layer alignment and overlap are checked during core laying. Cross-grain orientation is verified to ensure structural integrity.

Hot Press Monitoring

Pressure, temperature, and pressing time are continuously monitored. Test samples are pulled from each press cycle for bond strength verification.

Surface Finishing QC

Post-press sanding and film application are inspected for surface roughness, film adhesion, and edge sealing completeness.

Final Inspection & Packing

Random sampling from finished pallets checks thickness, size, moisture, and visual defects. Only certified lots are released for export.

Sustainability Commitment

Building Responsibly

At Born Wood, we recognize that sustainable business practices are essential for long-term success. We are committed to responsible sourcing, efficient production, and minimizing environmental impact across our operations.

  • Sourced from managed plantations and certified suppliers
  • Waste wood recycling into packaging-grade cores
  • Energy-efficient hot press systems
  • Water-based edge sealants to reduce VOC emissions
  • Compliance with local environmental regulations
