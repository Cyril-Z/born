3mm–6mm: Ideal for interior decoration, backing panels, and light packaging. 9mm–12mm: Suitable for furniture frames, cabinetry, and medium-duty packaging. 15mm–18mm: Recommended for concrete formwork, heavy furniture, and industrial shipping crates. Our film-faced construction plywood is engineered for multiple reuses. Depending on the thickness and handling conditions, 15mm–18mm boards typically achieve 15–25 pouring cycles with proper care and cleaning between uses.
Standard MOQ is 1×20ft container (approximately 20–22 cubic meters) per thickness/specification. Mixed container loading with multiple thicknesses is available to optimize your procurement.
